Sunrise Cafe

The Sunrise cafe name comes from the founder of the cafe who was described to come from his home which lied on a hill on the east of the town

During sunrise he would be seen as a vague figure from his home until he would descend then could one identify him.

This led to his nickname that was then incoprated as a family name Sunrise.

Surise then established a cafe which is located at the hill his humble abode once resided and because of its vagueness during sunrise, it carried on the name.

This name Sunrise stands even for its other braches around the country.
